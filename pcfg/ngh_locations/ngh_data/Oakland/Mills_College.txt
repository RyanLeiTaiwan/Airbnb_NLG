The chalet is located in a middle class neighborhood across from an all women's liberal arts college - Mills College. It is a 20-50 commute (car, BART or bus) into downtown San Francisco (depending on traffic and/or time of the day). It is just a 5-15 minute commute into Downtown Oakland and the shops and restaurants of Oakland's trendiest neighborhoods of Lake Merrit, Lakeshore, Temescal, and Rockridge.  It is also a quick commute (10-25 minutes) to Berkeley.
