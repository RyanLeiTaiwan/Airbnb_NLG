Charming Oakland Hills is conveniently located off highway 580 and just a 15-minute drive from downtown Oakland and San Leandro, Drake's Dealership Brewery, Drake's San Leandro Brewery, 1st Amendment Brewery, Oakland Zoo, great hiking spots, restaurants and bars. Quiet, residential neighborhood on top of a hill. You definitely need a car to get here, and public transportation (BART) is accessible by a 10-15 minute Uber or car ride.
There are a lot of trees!
This is a friendly family neighborhood filled with kids, dogs and long-time residents. It is quiet and safe residential neighborhood.
